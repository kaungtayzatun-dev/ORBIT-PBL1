The Challenges of Modern Traffic

Inefficient transit systems create a ripple effect of problems. Here is how traffic negatively impacts our environment, society, and human health.

Environmental Impact

Increased greenhouse gas emissions and poor air quality result from idle, congested vehicles.

Social & Urban Impact

Traffic jams lead to lost productivity, commuter stress, and delayed emergency response times.

Health Impact

Exposure to traffic-related pollutants like nitrogen dioxide and particulate matter is linked to asthma, reduced lung function, and chronic respiratory diseases.
