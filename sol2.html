Adaptive Signals

What Is It?

A dynamic traffic light control system that adjusts signal timing in real time based on actual traffic flow, rather than relying on static, pre-programmed schedules.

How It Works

  • Detection: Sensors monitor real-time vehicle flow and queue length.
  • Analysis: The system processes data to identify current intersection demand.
  • Optimization: AI algorithms determine the most efficient traffic light phase.
  • Adjustment: Traffic signals update in real-time to clear bottlenecks.

Why It Is Sustainable

It drastically reduces idling time, which lowers fuel consumption and greenhouse gas emissions. By optimizing flow, it maximizes the capacity of existing roads, delaying the need for environmentally damaging infrastructure expansions.
