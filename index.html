What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a vast network of physical objects embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity. These "smart" devices communicate, collect data, and exchange information with other systems over the internet, enabling automation, remote monitoring, and data-driven decision-making in everyday life.
