V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) Communication

What Is It?

A connectivity standard that allows vehicles to "talk" to other vehicles (V2V), infrastructure (V2I), pedestrians (V2P), and networks (V2N).

How It Works

  • Broadcasting: Vehicles and infrastructure constantly transmit status data.
  • Reception: Nearby units and vehicles receive this information instantly.
  • Processing: Onboard computers calculate potential conflicts or hazards.
  • Alerting/Action: The system warns drivers or modifies vehicle speed/path to ensure safety.

Why It Is Sustainable

Smoother vehicle-to-infrastructure interaction results in safer, more efficient driving with fewer hard-braking events and accidents, contributing to a more seamless and energy-efficient transport ecosystem.
